Customer Policy Guide

1. Introduction
   This Customer Policy Guide outlines the rules, expectations, and procedures governing customer interactions, purchases, returns, and support. It ensures transparency, fairness, and a consistent experience for all customers.

2. Account Registration Policy
   Customers must provide accurate and complete information during registration.
   Each user is responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.
   Multiple accounts created for fraudulent purposes may be suspended or permanently banned.

3. Payment Policy
   We accept payments via credit card, debit card, net banking, and digital wallets.
   All payments must be completed before order processing begins.

In case of payment failure:

* The order will not be confirmed.
* Customers may retry payment or choose another method.

4. Order Processing Policy
   Orders are processed within 1–3 business days.
   Customers will receive an order confirmation via email.

Delays may occur due to:

* High demand
* Inventory shortages
* External logistics issues

5. Shipping Policy
   Estimated delivery time: 3–7 business days.
   Shipping charges may vary based on location and order value.
   Customers will receive tracking details once the order is shipped.

6. Cancellation Policy
   Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement.
   Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.
   Refunds for canceled orders are processed within 5–7 business days.

7. Return and Refund Policy
   Customers can request a return within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for return:

* Product must be unused and in original packaging.
* Proof of purchase is required.

Refund process:

* Initiated after product inspection.
* Completed within 7–10 business days.

8. Exchange Policy
   Exchanges are allowed for defective or damaged products.
   Requests must be raised within 7 days of delivery.
   Replacement is subject to product availability.

9. Privacy Policy
   Customer data is collected only for order processing and service improvement.
   We do not sell or share personal data with third parties without consent.
   Data is stored securely and handled according to industry standards.

10. Customer Support Policy
    Support is available via:

* Email
* Chat
* Phone

Response time: Within 24 hours.
Customers must provide order ID for faster resolution.

11. Fraud Prevention Policy
    Suspicious transactions may be flagged for verification.
    Orders may be canceled if fraudulent activity is detected.
    Accounts involved in fraud will be permanently banned.

12. Dispute Resolution Policy
    Customers are encouraged to contact support for issue resolution.
    Disputes will be handled within 5 business days.
    Legal jurisdiction applies as per company registration location.

13. Policy Updates
    Policies may be updated periodically.
    Customers will be notified of major changes via email or website updates.
